Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept health insurance? +
Yes, we accept cashless treatment under most major insurance providers, and also offer EMI options for larger treatments like implants and braces.
Is teeth whitening safe for enamel? +
Our whitening treatments use dentist-supervised, enamel-safe formulations. We assess your oral health first to ensure whitening is right for you.
Do you treat young children? +
Absolutely — Dr. Priya Menon specializes in pediatric dentistry, using a gentle, play-based approach to keep kids comfortable.
What should I do in a dental emergency? +
Call our 24/7 emergency line immediately. For a knocked-out tooth, keep it moist (in milk or saline) and reach us within 30 minutes for the best chance of saving it.
How often should I visit for a checkup? +
We recommend a routine checkup and cleaning every 6 months for adults and children to catch issues early and maintain oral health.
